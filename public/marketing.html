How it works

Three steps to automatic time tracking

Connect your workspace and start tracking instantly — no code, no plugins, no browser extensions.

01

Connect Slack or Teams

One-click OAuth. StatusTime gets read-only access to presence data. Takes under 60 seconds.

02

We track status changes

Every time a user goes online or away, we log it. Members and guests are automatically classified.

03

See hours in real time

Your dashboard shows today's hours, weekly totals, timezones, and per-user breakdowns instantly.

04

Export for payroll

Download CSV reports, pro forma invoices, and timesheets for any date range. Perfect for client billing.
